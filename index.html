Our Go-to-Market Strategy

A strategic, two-phase approach to build a comprehensive and trusted network.

1

Phase 1: Build the Foundation

We begin by populating our platform with publicly available data from government hospitals. This provides immediate value to users without requiring initial partnerships.

Govt. Verified Partner Hospital

Clear tagging differentiates listed hospitals from official partners, building user trust through transparency.

2

Phase 2: Scale & Partner

With an established user base, we approach private hospitals and government health offices with a proven platform. We offer our premium dashboard and emergency response system to turn them into "smart hospitals," securing partnerships, contracts, and official recognition.

A Diversified Revenue Model

Our multi-stream approach ensures sustainable growth and long-term profitability.

Commission & Subscriptions

A percentage fee on bookings from private hospitals and recurring SaaS fees for access to our premium dashboard.

Premium Services & Grants

Small fees for value-added patient services and securing government grants for digitizing public health infrastructure.

Sponsorships (Future)

As our user base grows, sponsored listings for hospitals, labs, and pharmacies will provide an additional revenue stream.
